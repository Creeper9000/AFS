How To Play?

When you first start the game, you will find yourself in a "spawn" building. In the building, there is a map detailing the locations of the training areas, which advance your training power and speed significantly. You will also see 2 NPCs: one named Boom (who is one of the quest NPCs), and another named Wukong. Lastly, you will see 4 icons at the bottom of the screen. The icon for Physical Strength is a fist, Durability is a armour, chakara is a blue fire and sword skill is a sword.


If you press the corresponding number and left-click (with the exclusion of chakra, where you do NOT have to left click), this is your "basic" training without the use of "training spots". Training Spots are certain places that you can train in that have a certain "multiplier" that will stack with your own multiplier. In order to gain the effect of training spots, you must use its basic training action in it, with the exception of durability, which only requires you to stay in the spot. Please note that the sword does NOT have any training areas since the swords already have multipliers.


Bosses

Nine Tailed Fox

Difficulty:

Quite Easy

Boss Drops:

  • Tailed Mode
  • Spiral Shuriken
  • Multiple Bomb Spiral Shuriken
  • Tailed Monster Bomb

Boss Attacks

  • Swipe: Nine Tailed Fox swipes it's arm, dealing damage to whoever hit.
  • Slam: Nine Tailed Fox slams the ground, creating a translucent yellow shockwave. It knocks the player back while dealing damage.
  • Tailed Monster Bomb: Nine Tailed Fox will charge up a ball of energy at it's mouth, launching it towards the player.

Broly

Difficulty:

Hard

Boss Drops:

  • Legendary Super Human
  • Meteor Shower
  • Energy Barrage
  • Energy Burst

Boss Attacks

  • Punch: Broly punches the player, knoking them back and damaging them.
  • Energy Burst: Broly rises into the air. Absorbing green coloured energy before expelling it. The attack knocks players back and damages them over a large area.
  • Eraser Cannon: Broly will do a brief pose before launching a green beam instantly at a player.

Beast King

Difficulty:

Average

Boss Drops:

  • Barrier
  • Kings Outburst
  • Blade Whirlwind

Boss Attacks

  • Bite: Beast King bites infront of himself near ground level, doing damage to players hit.
  • Lightning: Beast King summons lighting bolts that surround him. If touched, players will be damaged.
  • Fire Beam: Beast King shakes it's head before launching a beam of fire at a player, damaging and knocking them back.

Masked Villain

Difficulty:

Hard

Boss Drops:

  • Air Cannon
  • Ultimate Power Combination
  • Springy Limbs
  • Impact Recoil

Boss Attacks

  • Punch: Masked Villain punches the player, knocking them back and damaging them.
  • Air Cannon: Masked Villain does a brief pose before launching a beam of air at a player, damaging them.
  • Ultimate Power Combination: Masked Villain does a brief pose before punching the ground creating a shockwave lasting for a while, knocking back and damaging player.

Beast Monke

Difficulty:

Average

Boss Drops:

  • Beast Titan Transformation
  • [Beast Titan]: Ground Smash
  • [Beast Titan]: Rock Throw

Boss Attacks

  • Slap: Beast Monke slaps infront of him, dealing damage.
  • Ground Smash: Beast Monke smashes the ground, creating a circle of debris. Whoever goes near or is in the circle will be damaged and knocked away.
  • Rock Throw: Beast Monke throws rock infront of him, dealing damage to people standing within the range of the rocks.
